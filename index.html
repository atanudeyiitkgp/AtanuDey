Atanu Dey
Researcher (Computational Linguistic)

“Research is to see what everybody else has seen, and to think what nobody else has thought.”- Albert Szent-Gyorgyi

He is an "E-Commerce Quality Data Scientist" at "HP Inc.", India, who loves to think unique & out of the box.

Atanu is currently working in "HP Inc." as an "E-Commerce Quality Data Scientist". He is doing research in the field of Computational Linguistics in broad.

He received MS (by research) degree from Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur (IIT-KGP) in 2018 on the topic of "Sentiment Analysis using n-gram Lexicon and Cross-Domain Features" under the supervision of Prof. Mamata Jenamani and Prof. Jitesh J Thakkar. He also received B. Tech. in "Computer Science and Engineering" in 2014 from Govt. College of Engineering and Textile Technology, Berhampore, West Bengal, India.

His research ranges widely across computational linguistics; special interests include natural language processing (NLP), deep learning (DL) and the application of NLP and DL to the social and behavioral sciences. Atanu was born and grew up in Howrah, India. He currently lives in Bengaluru, India.
